Description of Services

Digsy

Digsy is an iOS app that helps you track apartments you tour in New York City and New Jersey. It keeps a private record of every building and unit, calculates net-effective rent after concessions, provides a tour checklist, and lets you email leasing agents and import listing details. Everything you enter stays on your device unless you opt into iCloud sync.

Pigeon

Pigeon is an iOS app built for casual Citi Bike riders in New York City. It shows live bike and dock counts, warns you before a destination dock fills, keeps a diary of your rides, offers routing along bike lanes, and includes a live voice Talk feature between riding partners.
