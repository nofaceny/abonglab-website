Privacy Policy

Last updated July 16, 2026

This policy explains what information Pigeon collects, how it is used, and how you can control it. Pigeon is built by Abonglab. By using Pigeon you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Location. Pigeon asks for your location only while the app is in use. It is used to find nearby stations, record the path of your ride, and give you directions.
  • Health and fitness data. If you allow it, Pigeon reads your existing cycling workouts from Apple Health to show them in your ride history, and writes each completed ride back to Apple Health as a cycling workout.
  • Microphone and voice. If you use the Talk feature, Pigeon accesses your microphone so you can speak with your riding partner. Voice audio is carried live through LiveKit, a third-party real-time communication service, only for the length of an active Talk session. Pigeon does not record or store your voice audio.
  • Device identifier. Pigeon does not require an account or login. Each device generates a random identifier that is used to connect your rides, saved places, and preferences to your device. This identifier is stored securely on your device.
  • Ride and usage data. Pigeon stores the rides you record, including route, distance, and duration, along with any places or trails you save or publish, so your ride history and diary stay available to you.
  • Push notifications. If you enable notifications, Pigeon registers your device with Apple's push notification service so it can alert you when a dock near your destination is filling up.
  • Purchases. If you subscribe to Pigeon Pro, your purchase is handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. Pigeon never sees or stores your payment card details.

How we use information

We use the information above to operate Pigeon's core features, to send the dock alerts you opt into, to maintain your ride history and diary, and to keep the app secure and working correctly. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell your information to anyone.

Who we share data with

Pigeon shares data only with the services below, each used strictly to operate the app.

  • Apple. Handles purchases, push notifications, and Health data, all governed by Apple's own privacy policy.
  • Fly.io. Hosts Pigeon's backend server and database.
  • LiveKit. Carries live voice audio during an active Talk session. It does not receive your location or ride history.
  • Stadia Maps. Receives route coordinates to calculate bike directions. It does not receive your device identifier.

Data retention and deletion

Your ride history and saved places remain on Pigeon's server for as long as you use the app. If you would like your data deleted, contact us at the email below and we will remove it within thirty days.

Your choices

You can turn off location, microphone, Health access, or notifications at any time in your device settings. Deleting the app removes the identifier and any ride cache stored locally on your device.

Children's privacy

Pigeon is not directed at children under thirteen. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
