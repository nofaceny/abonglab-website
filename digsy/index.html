Privacy Policy

Last updated July 16, 2026

This policy explains what information Digsy handles and how it is used. Digsy is built by Abonglab. In short: Digsy does not collect your data, does not require an account, and keeps everything you enter on your own device. By using Digsy you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

We do not collect your personal data. Digsy has no account or login, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. The information you create in the app lives on your device, and Abonglab never receives it. Specifically:

  • Your tours. The buildings, units, rents, notes, checklist answers, and walkthrough videos you add are stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device database. They are never sent to Abonglab.
  • iCloud (optional). If you turn on iCloud sync in Settings, your tours are kept in step across your own Apple devices through your private iCloud account. This data is stored in your iCloud, governed by Apple's privacy policy, and Abonglab cannot read it.
  • Location. If you allow it, Digsy uses your location only while the app is in use, on your device, to suggest the building you are standing in front of and to estimate commute times. Your location is never sent to Abonglab.
  • Camera, microphone, and photos. If you use them, Digsy records walkthrough videos or imports existing ones, entirely on your device. This media never leaves your device unless you choose to share it yourself.
  • Purchases. If you buy Digsy Pro, the purchase is handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. Abonglab never sees or stores your payment information.

Maps

Digsy uses Apple Maps to search for addresses, place pins, and estimate travel times. These requests are handled by Apple under Apple's own privacy policy. When you choose to import details from a listing link, Digsy fetches that public web page directly, and no personal information is sent in the request.

How we use information

Because Digsy keeps your information on your device, that information is used only to run the app's features for you: tracking your tours, doing the rent math, showing your buildings on a map, and syncing across your own devices if you enable iCloud. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell your information to anyone.

Data retention and deletion

Your data stays on your device for as long as you keep the app. Deleting the app removes everything stored locally. If you enabled iCloud sync, you can remove that data from your device's iCloud settings at any time, and it is managed entirely by your own Apple account.

Your choices

You can turn off location access at any time in your device settings, and you can turn iCloud sync on or off whenever you like. Because there is no account, deleting the app is a complete and immediate way to remove your local data.

Children's privacy

Digsy is not directed at children under thirteen, and because Digsy collects no data, we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.

Questions about this policy? Contact privacy@abonglab.com.
