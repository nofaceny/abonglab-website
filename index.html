Digsy

Your apartment tour tracker

Digsy keeps a private record of every building and unit you tour, so you never lose track of what you saw or what it really costs. Tuned for New York City and New Jersey, and it works wherever you are hunting.

  • 📋 Tour Tracker: A private log of every building and unit you visit
  • 💵 Real Rent Math: Net-effective rent after concessions, calculated for you
  • ✅ Tour Checklist: The questions worth asking, built right in
  • 🔗 Smart Import: Fill in details from a listing link, email, or screenshot
  • ⇄ Side-by-Side Compare: Line up your top picks and decide with confidence

Pigeon

Your Citi Bike companion

Pigeon shows live bike and dock counts across New York City, warns you before your destination fills up, and keeps a lasting diary of every ride you take.

  • 🚲 Live Counts: Bikes and open docks at every station, updated live
  • ⚠️ Dock Alerts: A heads-up before your destination dock fills
  • 📔 Ride Diary: A lasting record of every ride and the city you've explored
  • 🗺️ Smart Routing: Safe, scenic, and fast routes along real bike lanes
  • 🎙️ Talk: Live voice with a riding partner while you both ride
